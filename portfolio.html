Gold Award - 2018



I earned a Gold Award in the year 2018 from Greater Girl Scouts of New York.

The Gold Award is the highest award a girl scout can earn. It is given to those who have made a project that has made a long lasting impact on the community.

Being a high school senior and having some interest in computer science, I decided to make a website spreading awareness of bullying.

I have presented it to elementary school students and to the younger generations in my girl scout troop; informing them the dangers of bullying.
